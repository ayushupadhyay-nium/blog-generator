# Writing Guidelines

## Voice & Tone
- Lead with narrative. Every point should feel like part of a story.
- Use contractions freely (it's, you're, we've). Feels like conversation.
- Assume no prior knowledge. Explain every concept on first mention.

## Article Structure
- Place an H2 every 300 words to help scanners navigate.

## Sentences & Paragraphs
- Max sentence length: 20 words. If longer, split it.
- Max 3 sentences per paragraph. Short paragraphs aid scanning.

## SEO
- Place the primary keyword naturally within the first 100 words.
